Dear colleagues, dear friends, dear family,

What is this all about?
The tradition around PhD Defense is that a very attentive colleague, friend, family member will organize a secret fundraising to buy some present to the PhD Candidate.

As some of you may know, this tradition does not match my minimalist journey as a non-consumer advocate. Therefore, I need your help to accomplish some dear project of mine.

What kind of project?
If you understand my journey to minimalism and life-long learning, I invite you to help me collect the funding necessary to the next training I am applying for: Embodied Facilitator Training.

It is a one-year training with 2 residencies is to deepen my own embodied wisdom and learn how to guide others in their journey. It's an opportunity to connect to a supportive, diverse community of change-makers, community-builders and arse-kickers. It's also a scary journey to push myself once more out of my comfort zone.

Who am I and why this Course?
An independent researcher and a fulltime self-learner. A problem solver with efficient playfulness. A professional swiss army knife. But mostly, I am a mover, a moral authority against laziness and security seekers, as well as an out of comfort zone and out of the box explorer.

As an academic, mind matters. As an acrobat, body matters. But that is a simplistic way fof describing things. In the past years, I discovered the importance to train people on the interconnection between body and mind. In this line, I am applying for this course for its strong ethics and for the multidisciplinary perspective it embraces. As such, it is a precious link to my current work at AIM & Associés and my current passion for Acrobatics.

Partner Movement Acro and Yoga

Course description: ‘Partner-Movement: Yoga & Acro with Iulia and Dori’ is an inspiring class proposed by Iulia Grosman and Dorota Myszkorowska. It offers instructive, technical, creative and playful approach to movement through yoga and acro inspired practices. In this class, you will practice conditioning through breathing, a modified primary series of yoga, partner movement and trust exercises. For beginners, Yoga and Acroyoga elements will open a new world of practice safely. For intermediate practitioners, their technical approach to the practice will consolidate your knowledge but for sure challenge your limits. The purpose of the class is to secure the idea that “you can do much more than you think”.

• Level: The class is beginner-friendly.
• Schedule: every Tuesday from 7:45 to 8:45.
• Price: Free of charge
• Registration: To register for class and sign up, complete and update each week the doodle or contact Iulia (Doodle Link, Email: iulia.grosman@gmail.com)
• Location: Rue d’Arlon 62, eighth floor, room BMT 8A090, 1000 Bruxelles
• Audience and access: the class is primarily directed for the Institutions. You will need a badge to access the building. However, you may contact us in order to invite additional explorers.

About the docents: Iulia Grosman (acroyogi, handstander, yogi, amateur acrobat, movement practicionner but also...linguistic researcher, wellbeing trainer and new technology coordinator) & Dorota Myszkorowska (acroyogi, yogi, contemporary and belly dancer, fitness coach, amateur runner but also ... assistant in the European Parliament and English language teacher) have discovered their common interest of playful interdisciplinarity several years ago. They create a safe space for creativity by giving a technical but yet playful approach. With this physical but deeply meditative practice, they will make sure you begin your day with a smile.
